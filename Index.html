Environmental Impact at a Glance

Deforestation for Human Activity

10,000 km²

Forests are cleared annually for urbanization, agriculture, and mining. This leads to habitat loss for countless species.

Carbon Emissions

5 Billion Tons

CO₂ emissions are released annually due to deforestation. Trees act as carbon sinks, and their loss accelerates global warming.

Forest Fires

200+ Incidents

Forest fires are reported each year, destroying thousands of hectares and affecting air quality worldwide.

Our Goal: Save the Forests

We're dedicated to combating deforestation, reducing carbon emissions, and protecting natural habitats for wildlife. Your contribution can make a big difference in preserving our planet for future generations.
